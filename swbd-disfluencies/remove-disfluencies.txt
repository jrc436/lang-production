Disfluency Removal in Switchboard
remove-disfluencies.perl



EDITED   (disfluencies - edited part (i.e. reparandum))
PRN  (interjections, "you know", "I guess")
RM/IP/RS / -DFL -   (disfluency markup)

... are removed completely. 

INTJ: Hesitations ("uh") are removed. So are any predecing or following commas


TYPO: Only the TYPO mark is removed, so the wrongly spelled word including the possibly wrong tag is inserted.

-UNF: Utterances containing unfinished constituents are dropped from the analysis.
